About Us

The Agricultural Statistics Office of the Department of Agriculture serves as the strategic driver for unified data governance, the integration of information systems that support data extraction for management use, and the application of big data analytics to enhance planning, policy formulation, and service delivery in the agriculture and fisheries sector.

DA AgriStat Hub consolidates dashboards, geospatial maps, and raw data sources into a single, accessible portal — empowering policymakers, researchers, and other stakeholders with timely agricultural intelligence.

Embedded Dashboards

Full-page interactive dashboards from Looker Studio and Power BI, updated in real time.

Geospatial Maps

Spatial agricultural data for regional and provincial analysis and visualization.

Standardized Datasets

PSA-aligned commodity classifications and DA operational reported data.

Official & Trusted

Authoritative data sourced directly from the PSA, DA Operational Units (OU) and regional field operations nationwide.
